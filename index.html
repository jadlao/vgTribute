Vincent Van Gogh

Post-impressionist painter

Here's a timeline of Vincent Van Gogh's life:


  • 1853 - Born in Groot-Zundert, Netherlands
  • 1864 - 1866 - Went to boarding school in Zevenbergen
  • 1869 - Worked at the Hague Gallery
  • 1876 - 1880 - Van Gogh begins devoting his life to the evangelization of the poor in Borinage, Belgium
  • 1885 - Completes "The Potato Eaters"
  • 1886 - Moves to Paris and discovers Impressionists and Post-Impressionists
  • 1887 - Completes self-portrait with straw hat
  • 1888 - Completes the famous sunflower pieces and begins suffering from mental problems
  • 1888 - Completes "Starry Night Over the Rhone"
  • 1888 - Van Gogh cuts off a portion of his ear and admits himself to an insane asylum
  • 1889 - Completes "Starry Night"
  • 1890 - Completes "Almond Blossom"
  • 1889 - Dies of a self-inflicted gunshot
